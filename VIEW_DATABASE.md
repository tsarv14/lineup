# How to View Your Database in MongoDB Atlas

## Step-by-Step Guide:

### Step 1: Log In to MongoDB Atlas
1. Go to https://cloud.mongodb.com
2. Log in with your MongoDB Atlas account

### Step 2: Find Your Cluster
1. You should see your cluster (the database you created)
2. It might say something like "Cluster0" or have a name you gave it
3. Click on it

### Step 3: View Your Collections
**Option A: Using Collections Tab**
1. Look for a tab that says **"Collections"** (at the top)
2. Click on it
3. You should see your database name (probably "vibely" or "lineup")
4. Click on the database name
5. You'll see all your collections (users, storefronts, plans, etc.)

**Option B: Using Database Access**
1. Look for **"Database"** in the left sidebar
2. Click on it
3. Click **"Browse Collections"** or **"Collections"**
4. Select your database
5. You'll see all collections

**Option C: Using the Cluster View**
1. Click on your cluster name
2. Look for a button that says **"Browse Collections"** or **"Collections"**
3. Click it
4. Select your database
5. View your collections

### Step 4: View Your Data
1. Click on a collection name (like "users")
2. You'll see all the documents (data) in that collection
3. Each document is one user, subscription, etc.
